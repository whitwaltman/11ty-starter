Once you've covered the [basics](/basics), you can start creating content and customizing the look and feel of your site. {% note %}`_includes/css/base.css` contains most of the style rules, so you might want to start there{% endnote %}

If you don't know what to make, I keep a running list of [things you could do or make](/ideas) here, or check out [this list](https://jamesg.blog/2024/02/19/personal-website-ideas) for even more ideas. Have fun with it! Make it weird!
